Frequently Asked Questions

How does the booking process work?

Select your preferred celebrity and experience type, choose an available date, and complete the secure payment process. You'll receive confirmation and detailed instructions immediately.

What if the celebrity needs to reschedule?

We offer flexible rescheduling options or full refunds if the celebrity needs to change the appointment. Your satisfaction is guaranteed.

Are the meetings private and secure?

Yes, all virtual meetings use end-to-end encryption, and in-person meetings include professional security measures.

What's included in a virtual meet?

Virtual meets include a private video call, digital photo opportunities, and the option to record the session (with mutual consent). You can also request digital autographs and personalized messages.

How long are the meetings?

Virtual meetings typically last 15-20 minutes, while in-person meetings range from 30-60 minutes depending on the package selected. Duration is clearly specified during booking.

Can I bring items for autographs?

Yes, for in-person meetings you can bring up to two items for autographs. This should be specified during booking to ensure the celebrity is prepared.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels.

Is there an age requirement?

Users must be 18+ to book independently. Minors can participate with parent/guardian consent and supervision.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before the scheduled meeting. Within 48 hours, a 50% fee applies unless due to celebrity rescheduling.

Can I gift a celebrity meet to someone?

Yes! We offer gift certificates and the ability to book on behalf of others. The recipient will receive special instructions and a personalized message.

What happens if technical issues occur?

Our technical support team is available during all virtual meets. If issues can't be resolved, we'll reschedule or refund your session.

Are group bookings available?

Yes, we offer special rates for group bookings and fan club events. Contact our support team for custom packages.
